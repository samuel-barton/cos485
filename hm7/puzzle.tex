\section*{Problem 7}

In this problem we are asked to find a graph which would force the \textit{Alphabetical Ford-Fulkerson Algorithm} to create at least $E + 1$ augmenting paths.
\\
\\
The graph below will require $2 \cdot N$ traversals of which $N$ are augmenting
paths. The problem statement tells us that there must be $E + 1$ augmenting
paths and thus we must have $N = E + K$ where $K \geq 1$. 
\\
