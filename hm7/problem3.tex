\section*{Problem 3}

In this problem we are to find a set of augmenting paths which will 
make the flow in Figure \ref{base-flow} maximal. 
\\
\\

\begin{center}

    \textcolor{cyan}{\textbf{S --- D --- E --- C --- B --- T}} & 6 \\
    \end{tabular}
\end{center}
